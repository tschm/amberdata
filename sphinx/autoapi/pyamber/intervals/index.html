Constructs an iterator (start_date, t2), (t2, t3), …, (t_{n-1}, end_date). This is used to address the pagination of amberdata.

Parameters

  • start_date – First date

  • end_date – Last date

  • freq – Gap between Dates (this parameter is induced by amberdata)
